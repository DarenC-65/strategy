Welcome

Thank you for taking part in this survey of Pennsylvania K-12 school district superintendents. Your responses will help build an accurate picture of how district leaders across the Commonwealth understand artificial intelligence (AI), how districts are currently using it, what concerns leaders have, and where AI might help improve education, operations, or persistent district challenges.

Results will be reported in aggregate only. Individual responses and district names will not be published or shared. Naming your district is optional.

There are no "right" answers. Skeptical, cautious, and enthusiastic perspectives are all equally valuable. Most questions are optional — answer what you can.

The survey takes about 12–15 minutes. When you finish, your answers will download as a small file that you'll email back to us (instructions provided at the end).
